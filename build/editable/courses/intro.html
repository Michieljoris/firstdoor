Nationally Recognised Training

First Door is registered with the Australian Skills Quality Authority as a Registered Training Organisation (RTO) to provide competency based and Nationally Recognised Training. 

We are currently enrolling students to commence training in the new CHC50113 Diploma of Early Childhood Education and Care. This Diploma course commences on 3 March, 2014 and rolling enrolments are taken thereafter. During the enrolment process each student will commence either a Structured or Flexi learning plan to best support their success.

Options are also available for those that may have already completed a qualification, and want to continue developing their professional skills.

Enrol in a competency based workshop for your professional development

Become inspired, and develop your capability by enrolling in one or more of our interactive, competency based workshops.

Workshops engage educators and leaders in active learning, based on the knowledge and skills required in each unit. Each workshop relates to the Early Childhood National Quality Framework and introduces strategies relevant to your workplace. Morning tea is supplied and all participants receive a certificate of attendance.

Bookings and fees for a competency based workshop

Bookings are required by emailing admin@firstdoor.com.au or phoning 3103 2336. Payment is to be made prior to the workshop to confirm your place, as places are limited. Your workshop investment is $75 per person, or $55 per person for bookings of two or more people.
 

Take on a unit of competency  

Attend the workshop and then complete an assessment workbook to complete a unit of competency. On successful completion students receive a Statement of Attainment of a Nationally Recognised unit of competency. A First Door enrolment form is to be completed to undertake one or more units of competency.

Enrolment and fees for a unit of competency
Download an enrolment form here, or request by email or phone. Your first unit fee is $235 to include a one-off $40 enrolment fee. Second and further units are charged at $195 each. The investment for each unit of competency includes:

  • the workshop fee
  • a printed theory book
  • study support
  • personal mentoring, and
  • constructive assessment

Further options of study are available to extend leadership and management capability by completing units provided from:

  • TAE40110 Certificate IV in Training and Assessment
  • BSB51107 Diploma of Management
