Montessori

Key concepts shown in this video show the Montessori approach is not to fill the child with facts but to cultivate the child’s love of learning. The Montessori key is looking for what grabs the child’s interest and enthusiasm, and aims to enable each child to reach their full potential. Montessori believes the child’s hands are their first teacher. The materials are self-corrective and are designed for experimentation, The environment is organised and is non-competitive. Children are encouraged to be responsible, learn life skills and care for their environment.
