Rudolf Steiner

This video shows the Rudolph Steiner approach with daily rhythm in routines, open ended play with a focus on using the child’s hands/body and provided an atmosphere of real care. The curriculum is rich and structured, it relates to the children’s stage of development. It embraces different cultures and understanding of the world. The objective is to provide a great breadth of education to educate the whole child to become free thinking, responsible adults.

Discussion point: How does the Steiner approach relate to the National Quality Framework? How do we provide in some of these approaches?
