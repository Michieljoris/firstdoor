Arctic Norway outdoor kindergarten

Nearly one in ten kindergartens in Norway are Outdoor Kindergartens. Despite freezing weather, the trend is to return young children to nature and outdoor air.

Discussion point: How does an Outdoor Kindergarten provide for the learning outcomes such as: developing resilience, creativity, curiosity and physical skills? What implications would an Outdoor Kindergarten have on children’s behaviour?
