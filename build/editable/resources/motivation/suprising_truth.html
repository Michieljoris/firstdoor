The surprising truth about what motivates us by Dan Pink Motiivation

Motivation is not just about the money. Major motivators for people are operating with a sense of autonomy, achieving mastery and having purpose.


Discussion point: It follows that motivation will be lowered when we are directed by others, feel incapable and lack in purpose. How does your organisation develop a sense of purpose and autonomy in its people? Does it provide opportunities for people to practise and master skills?
